About me

I am a junior front-end developer passionate about code, design, and lifelong learning. After 7 months of full immersion, intensive studies, research, and building sites with HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript, I am now determined to achieve excellence, to deliver a positive contribution and to be part of an ambitious team.
Creative problem solving, and implementation of design solutions for enhanced user experience is what fascinates me foremost in web development. It embodies an exciting combination of logic and creativity, enabling to take something abstract such as a programming language and turn it into something tangible as a website or an application.
I accord a great deal of importance to aesthetics, improvement, clean design and understanding the logic behind a piece of code that I am about to implement. I am self-disciplined, analytical, attentive to details, whilst imaginative, creative and flexible. I am equally comfortable working in French or in English, in a team or autonomously.
Besides coding, I love to get lost in places I have never been to, practice Ashtanga yoga, read, dance and when I have the opportunity to do some scuba diving.
If you are in search of a highly motivated junior developer to join your team, get in contact with me! I would be delighted.
